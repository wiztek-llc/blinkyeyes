What is the 20-20-20 rule?

A simple habit that protects your eyes from screen fatigue.

Every 20 minutes

Take a short break from your screen

Look 20 feet away

Focus on something in the distance

For 20 seconds

Give your eyes a moment to relax

Recommended by the American Academy of Ophthalmology to reduce digital eye strain.

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Simple, thoughtful, and out of your way.

Non-intrusive reminders

A gentle overlay slides down from the top of your screen. No popups, no focus stealing, no interruptions.

Analytics dashboard

Track your streaks, compliance rate, and daily habits. See how consistent you've been over the last week.

System tray app

Runs quietly in your system tray. You'll forget it's there — until it's time for a break.

Customizable

Set your own work intervals, break durations, and daily goals. Toggle notifications, sounds, and themes.

Idle-aware

Blinky knows when you step away. No wasted reminders while you're grabbing coffee.

Lightweight

~3MB download. ~15MB RAM. Built with Tauri, not Electron. Your laptop battery will thank you.
